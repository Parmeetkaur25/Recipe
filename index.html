INSTANT POT SPAGHETTI

My whole family loves this instant pot spaghetti!
Silky , flavourful , and delightfully clingy.Easuy enough for my kids to amke it too!


Here's a quick recipe for instant hot spaghetti: Ingredients:
Boil Water: In a pot, bring 4 cups of water to a boil. Add a pinch of salt. Cook Spaghetti: Add the spaghetti to the boiling water and cook according to the package instructions (usually about 8-10 minutes) until al dente. Drain: Once cooked, drain the spaghetti in a colander. Mix with Sauce: Return the spaghetti to the pot, add your preferred sauce, and mix well. You can also add a splash of olive oil for extra flavor. Serve: Plate the spaghetti, and top with grated cheese and fresh herbs if desired. Enjoy your meal!

CHOCOLATE CHERRY SMOOTHIE

This chocolate cherry smoothie is like a healthy milkshake!
Creamy, dreamy, and packed with antioxidants.


Here's a simple recipe for a chocolate cherry smoothie: Blend Ingredients: In a blender, combine the frozen cherries, banana, almond milk, cocoa powder, and honey or maple syrup if using. Blend Until Smooth: Blend on high until all ingredients are well combined and the mixture is smooth. If you prefer a thicker smoothie, add ice cubes and blend again. Serve: Pour into a glass and enjoy your delicious chocolate cherry smoothie!

CALORIES:-400
